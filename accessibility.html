Website Accessibility

Our Commitment

Jivamukti Yoga is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all visitors, regardless of ability or technology. We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level, and we continuously work to improve the accessibility and usability of our digital presence.

What We Do

We design our pages with semantic HTML, provide alternative text for images, ensure sufficient color contrast, support keyboard navigation, and use ARIA landmarks where appropriate. Our forms include clear labels and error messages. We regularly test our site with assistive technologies and address any issues that are identified.

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our website or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at info@jivamuktiyoga.com. We welcome your feedback and will do our best to respond promptly and make necessary adjustments.
