Common Questions

What is the difference between Open and Spiritual Warrior?

The Open class is creatively sequenced by the teacher with detailed instruction. Spiritual Warrior is a fixed one-hour sequence with minimal instruction — perfect for practitioners who want a consistent, meditative flow.

Do I need to be vegan to train?

While Jivamukti Yoga emphasizes ahimsa (non-violence) and ethical veganism, the training welcomes all sincere seekers. The program explores veganism as part of yoga philosophy and practice.

What books are required?

The core reading list includes: Jivamukti Yoga: Practices for Liberating Body and Soul, Art of Assists, Yoga and Veganism, The Jivamukti Chant Book, and The Magic Ten and Beyond.
