The Jivamukti Legacy

Continuing the path of liberation through compassion

Jivamukti Global

In 2017, Sharon Gannon and David Life retired from public life and entered into the vanaprastha stage (forest dwelling), in order to deepen their yogic sadhana. They entrusted the Jivamukti lineage into the capable hands of certified Jivamukti Yoga teachers, creating a new partnership called Jivamukti Global.

Jivamukti Global is the only entity authorized to conduct all Jivamukti Teacher Training courses, including the 300HR, 800HR apprentice program, Advanced Board, Immersion Course, and 75HR Teacher Training, as well as to authorize the opening of new Jivamukti Yoga studios.

Camilla Veen

Director — Jivamukti Global

Camilla founded Atha Yoga in Stavanger, Norway in 2005, which was transformed into a certified Jivamukti Yoga Center in 2017. She serves as director of the center and teaches Jivamukti yoga classes and workshops in Norway and internationally. The holistic perspective of Jivamukti Yoga appeals deeply to Camilla — with equal emphasis on spiritual, mental, and physical aspects of yoga.

Hari Mulukutla

Director — Jivamukti Global

Hari is deeply grateful to the teachers of Ananda Ashram and the disciples of Shri Brahmananda Sarasvati, including Sharon Gannon and David Life, who have enriched his spiritual journey with wisdom and love. At Sharon and David's request, Hari oversaw the Jivamukti Yoga School in New York during its final years until its closure. Camilla and Hari continue to oversee the operations and development of Jivamukti Global.
